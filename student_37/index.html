On this page, you will learn how to take care of a guinea pigs and also learn some fun tips and tricks!

If you have any further questions about guinea pigs, direct them to the local L.A. Guinea Pig Shelter.

Daily Guinea Pig Reutines

  • 1. Feed guinea pig with hay and "guinea pig," food.
  • 2. Clean the cage at least once a week.
  • 3. Make sure to get your guinea pig out of their cage everyday to keep it happy and friendly.:)
  • Feed your guinea pig additional foods like carrots, lettus(not iceberg, it will kill them), and spinach for a treat.
